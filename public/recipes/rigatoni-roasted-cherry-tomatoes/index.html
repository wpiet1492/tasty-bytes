Ingredients

  • 750 grams of halved cherry tomatoes
  • 4 peeled cloves of garlic
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 500 grams of rigatoni pasta
  • 140 grams of tomato puree
  • 50 ml whipped cream
  • some fresh basil
  • 300 grams burrata

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 240 degrees.
  2. Arrange the halved cherry tomatoes in a baking dish. Add garlic and season freshly ground black pepper. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
  3. Roast the cherry tomatoes and garlic in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, fry the onion for 10 minutes over medium heat in a pan.
  5. Cook the rigatoni pasta until al dente. Reserve a few tablespoons of the cooking water.
  6. After 10 minutes, add the tomato paste to the pan with the onion. SautÃ© for 2 minutes, then add the roasted cherry tomatoes and their juices.
  7. Add the reserved pasta cooking water, cream and torn basil leaves and bring gently to the boil.
  8. Simmer for 10 minutes over low heat.
  9. Stir in the cooked pasta.
  10. Spread the burrata over the pasta.
