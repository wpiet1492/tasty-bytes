## Ingredients

- 2 bananas
- 2 eggs
- 250g flour
- 500ml whole milk
- 150g Nutella

## Directions

1. Mash 1 banana with a fork and add the eggs, flour, milk, and salt. Whisk.
2. Bake the pancake in a pan with a drizzle of oil.
3. Spread each pancake with a tablespoon (approx. 15g) of Nutella.
4. Slice the second banana and arrange it among the spreads.
5. Tuck the sides of the pancake inward and roll them up.
